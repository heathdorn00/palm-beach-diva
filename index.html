✦ Meet Lucie

A World of Style,
Curated Just for You

I'm Lucie Burdova, world traveler, and your future best-dressed personal fashion stylist. Born in Prague, refined in Monte Carlo, and forever in love with Palm Beach, I've spent my life studying how the world's most stylish women dress — and how you can too.

After living across Monte Carlo, Milan, Prague, Boca Raton, and Las Vegas and traveling to over 90 countries, I've built a style philosophy that blends European elegance with American confidence. Style isn't about money — it's about knowing who you are and dressing accordingly.

My approach is personal, practical, and a little fabulous. Whether you need a total wardrobe overhaul or the perfect outfit for one unforgettable night, I'm here to make it happen.
