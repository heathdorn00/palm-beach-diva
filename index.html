Meet Lucie

Your Best-Dressed Friend Who's Been Everywhere

Born in Prague, shaped by years in Monte Carlo, refined on the shores of Palm Beach — Lucie Burdova has spent a lifetime inside the world's most stylish cities. With firsthand fashion experience spanning 90+ countries and 4 continents, she brings a rare global perspective to every styling session.

Lucie doesn't just dress women. She transforms how they carry themselves. She's lived the Palm Beach season, walked Worth Avenue, dressed for Monte Carlo galas and Italian summers. Now based in Las Vegas, she brings that same world-class eye to women who deserve to look extraordinary every day — not just on special occasions.

Whether you're preparing for a gala, refreshing a wardrobe that no longer feels like you, or building a signature look from scratch — Lucie sees exactly what you need. Her gift is bringing the diva out in every woman.
